## Recitals

Yalla Venao operates a technology-based delivery marketplace in Playa Venao, Veraguas Province, Republic of Panama. The Courier wishes to provide independent delivery services to customers placing orders through the Platform. The Parties enter into this Agreement to define their respective rights and obligations.

---

## 1. Independent Contractor Status

> ⚠️ **HIGH-RISK CLAUSE — CRITICALLY IMPORTANT**

### 1.1 No Employment Relationship

The Courier is an **independent contractor** and is **NOT an employee, agent, partner, or franchisee** of KANIEL TORD LLC or Yalla Venao. Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed to create an employment relationship, joint venture, or partnership.

**This means Yalla Venao:**
- Does **not** pay salary or wages
- Does **not** provide health insurance, social security, pension, or any other employment benefits
- Does **not** withhold taxes on the Courier's behalf
- Does **not** control the means, manner, or method of the Courier's work beyond delivery requirements
- Does **not** guarantee a minimum number of deliveries or income

### 1.2 Courier Autonomy

The Courier has the right to:
- Accept or decline any delivery request at their sole discretion
- Set their own working hours
- Work for other delivery platforms or services simultaneously
- Use any vehicle appropriate for the delivery (motorcycle, bicycle, car, etc.)

### 1.3 Tax Obligations

> ⚠️ The Courier is solely responsible for all taxes, social security contributions, and other fiscal obligations arising from their earnings as an independent contractor under the laws of the Republic of Panama. Yalla Venao will not withhold, file, or pay any taxes on the Courier's behalf. Couriers are advised to consult a Panamanian tax professional regarding their obligations.

---

## 2. No Employment Benefits

As an independent contractor, the Courier is **not entitled to** and will **not receive**:
- Salary or minimum wage guarantees
- Social security (Seguro Social / CSS) contributions from Yalla Venao
- Health insurance or medical benefits
- Paid vacation, sick leave, or public holidays
- Severance or termination payments under Panamanian labor law
- Workers' compensation coverage from Yalla Venao

> ⚠️ The Courier acknowledges that they are solely responsible for their own social security registration and contributions as an independent contractor (cuenta propia) under Panamanian law.

## 3. Earnings

### 3.1 Per-Delivery Rate

The Courier will earn a **per-delivery rate** as agreed with Yalla Venao at the time of onboarding. This rate will be communicated in writing (via WhatsApp or email) and may be updated with **14 days' notice** from Yalla Venao.

The current agreed per-delivery rate is: **[DELIVERY_RATE] (PAB/USD)**

### 3.2 Tips

Couriers may receive **cash tips** directly from customers. Tips are entirely voluntary and belong solely to the Courier. Yalla Venao does not facilitate, guarantee, or take any portion of tips.

### 3.3 No Minimum Earnings Guarantee

> ⚠️ Yalla Venao does **not** guarantee a minimum number of deliveries, minimum earnings per day/week/month, or any income floor. Earnings depend entirely on the number of orders accepted and completed.

### 3.4 Earnings Settlement

Yalla Venao will settle per-delivery earnings with the Courier on a **weekly or bi-weekly basis**, minus any amounts owed by the Courier to Yalla Venao (e.g., cash shortfalls). Payment is made via **Yappy** (Panama's mobile payment platform). The Courier must provide a valid Yappy-registered phone number at onboarding. If the Courier does not have a Yappy account, alternative arrangements must be agreed in writing.

## 4. No Minimum Hours Requirement

There are **no minimum hours or availability requirements**. The Courier may work as many or as few hours as they choose. Yalla Venao will not penalize Couriers for low activity, subject to Section 9 (Termination).

## 5. Courier Responsibilities

### 5.1 Vehicle & Equipment

The Courier agrees to:
- Provide and maintain their own vehicle (motorcycle, bicycle, car, etc.) in safe, roadworthy condition
- Ensure their vehicle carries valid registration and all required documentation under Panamanian law
- Bear all costs of vehicle maintenance, fuel, and repairs

### 5.2 Insurance

> ⚠️ **HIGH-RISK CLAUSE**

The Courier is **solely responsible** for obtaining and maintaining:
- Valid vehicle insurance as required by Panamanian law (SOAT or equivalent)
- Any additional liability insurance deemed necessary for delivery activities

**Yalla Venao does NOT provide vehicle insurance, liability coverage, or accident coverage for Couriers. In the event of an accident, injury, or property damage, the Courier bears sole responsibility. Yalla Venao expressly disclaims all liability for incidents occurring during delivery activities.**

### 5.3 Valid Licenses

The Courier must hold a valid Panamanian driver's license (or applicable license) appropriate for their vehicle type at all times during active deliveries.

### 5.4 Timely Delivery

The Courier agrees to:
- Accept delivery assignments they intend to fulfill
- Collect orders promptly from vendors upon notification
- Deliver orders to customers efficiently and within reasonable timeframes
- Communicate proactively with the customer and/or Yalla Venao if there are delays or issues

### 5.5 Professional Conduct

The Courier agrees to:
- Treat customers, vendors, and Yalla Venao staff with respect and professionalism
- Not consume alcohol or drugs while on delivery duty
- Represent the Yalla Venao brand positively during deliveries
- Wear or display any Yalla Venao identification or branding materials provided (if applicable)
- Not engage in harassment, discrimination, or inappropriate conduct toward any party

### 5.6 Order Integrity

The Courier agrees to:
- Collect orders as packaged by the vendor and deliver them in the same condition
- Not open, tamper with, or consume any portion of customer orders
- Handle food orders with care to prevent spills, damage, or contamination
- Report any damaged, missing, or incorrect orders to Yalla Venao immediately

## 6. Cash Handling Responsibilities

> ⚠️ **HIGH-RISK CLAUSE**

### 6.1 Cash Collection

The Courier is responsible for collecting the **full cash payment** from the customer upon delivery. The amount due is communicated by the Platform at the time of order assignment.

### 6.2 Accurate Cash Handling

The Courier agrees to:
- Collect the correct amount from the customer
- Provide change where applicable
- Not pocket, divert, or misappropriate any cash

### 6.3 Shortfalls & Disputes

> ⚠️ In the event of a cash shortfall (customer non-payment, short payment, or Courier error), the Courier agrees to report the issue to Yalla Venao immediately. Documented shortfalls due to Courier negligence or misconduct may be deducted from the Courier's future earnings.

Yalla Venao is **not responsible for non-payment by customers**. The Courier assumes the risk of failed cash collection in cases outside their reasonable control, provided they have followed proper procedures and reported the issue promptly.

### 6.4 Fraud

> ⚠️ Any confirmed fraudulent cash handling by the Courier (e.g., keeping customer payment, falsifying delivery) is grounds for **immediate termination** of this Agreement and may be reported to Panamanian authorities.

## 9. Term & Termination

### 9.1 Term

This Agreement begins on the date signed and continues until terminated by either Party.

### 9.2 Termination by Either Party

Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time by providing **7 days' written notice** via WhatsApp or email. No reason is required.

### 9.3 Immediate Termination for Cause

Yalla Venao may terminate this Agreement **immediately and without notice** if the Courier:
- Misappropriates cash payments
- Engages in fraud, theft, or criminal conduct
- Harasses or threatens customers, vendors, or Platform staff
- Delivers while under the influence of alcohol or drugs
- Causes harm or injury through gross negligence or willful misconduct
- Provides false documentation during onboarding

### 9.4 Effect of Termination

Upon termination:
- Yalla Venao will pay any outstanding per-delivery earnings owed within **14 days**
- The Courier must return any Yalla Venao branded materials
- Any cash shortfalls or amounts owed to Yalla Venao will be deducted from final payment
