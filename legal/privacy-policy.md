## 10. GDPR — Rights of EU/EEA Users

This section applies to you if you are a resident of the European Union or European Economic Area.

### 10.1 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

| Basis | Processing Activity |
|---|---|
| **Contract Performance** | Order fulfillment, account management |
| **Legitimate Interest** | Fraud prevention, platform security, analytics |
| **Consent** | WhatsApp notifications (you can withdraw at any time) |
| **Legal Obligation** | Compliance with applicable laws |

### 10.2 Your GDPR Rights

As an EU/EEA resident, you have the following rights:

#### Right of Access (Art. 15 GDPR)
You may request a complete copy of all personal data we hold about you.

#### Right to Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

#### Right to Erasure / "Right to be Forgotten" (Art. 17 GDPR)
You may request deletion of your personal data. We will honor this request unless we are required to retain data for legal or contractual obligations.

#### Right to Restriction of Processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
You may request that we restrict processing of your personal data in certain circumstances (e.g., while a dispute is being resolved).

#### Right to Data Portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
You may request your personal data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format (e.g., CSV or JSON). This applies to data you provided to us and that we process by automated means.

#### Right to Object (Art. 21 GDPR)
You may object to processing based on our legitimate interests. We will cease processing unless we can demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds.

#### Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7 GDPR)
Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
