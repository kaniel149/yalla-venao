## Recitals

Yalla Venao operates a technology-based delivery marketplace that connects customers with local vendors and independent couriers in Playa Venao, Veraguas Province, Republic of Panama. The Vendor wishes to list its products and services on the Platform. The Parties enter into this Agreement to define their respective rights and obligations.

## 1. Independent Contractor Status

> ⚠️ **HIGH-RISK CLAUSE — CRITICALLY IMPORTANT**

### 1.1 No Employment Relationship

The Vendor is an **independent contractor** and is **NOT an employee, agent, partner, or franchisee** of KANIEL TORD LLC or Yalla Venao. Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed to create an employment relationship, joint venture, or partnership between the Parties.

### 1.2 Vendor Autonomy

The Vendor retains full control over:
- Its business operations, staff, and hours
- Menu content, pricing, and product offerings
- Food preparation methods and kitchen management
- Its own branding and marketing (off-Platform)

### 1.3 Tax Obligations

> ⚠️ The Vendor is solely responsible for all taxes, levies, and contributions arising from its business activities and income, including applicable Panamanian business taxes, ITBMS (sales tax), and any other fiscal obligations. Yalla Venao will not withhold taxes on behalf of the Vendor.

### 1.4 No Benefits

Yalla Venao does not provide the Vendor with any employment benefits, including health insurance, social security, workers' compensation, or pension contributions.

## 2. No Exclusivity

The Vendor is **free to list and sell its products and services on other platforms, marketplaces, and delivery services** simultaneously with its participation on Yalla Venao. Yalla Venao does not require exclusivity. The Vendor may also conduct direct sales independent of the Platform.

---

## 3. Commission Structure

### 3.1 Platform Commission

Yalla Venao charges a commission of **15% of the total order value** (excluding any delivery fee) for each order successfully fulfilled through the Platform.

> **Example:** A customer orders $20 worth of food. Yalla Venao's commission = $3.00. The Vendor retains $17.00.

### 3.2 Order Value Definition

"Total order value" means the subtotal of all items in the customer's order as listed on the Platform, before any applicable discounts or adjustments agreed to between Vendor and Customer.

### 3.3 Commission Invoicing

> ⚠️ Commission invoicing and payment schedule will be agreed upon separately between the Parties in writing. Yalla Venao will provide the Vendor with a periodic summary of orders and applicable commissions owed.

### 3.4 No Upfront Fees

Yalla Venao charges no upfront listing fees, onboarding fees, or monthly subscription fees to Vendors at this time. The commission is the sole fee.

---

## 4. Payment & Cash Handling

### 4.1 Cash-Only Model

The Platform currently operates on a **cash-on-delivery basis**. Customers pay the courier directly in cash at the time of delivery. Yalla Venao does **not** collect, process, or hold customer payments on behalf of the Vendor.

### 4.2 Vendor Payment Collection

Vendors are responsible for tracking their order revenue. Commission amounts owed to Yalla Venao will be settled according to the agreed invoicing schedule.

> ⚠️ Yalla Venao does not guarantee payment collection from customers on behalf of Vendors. Any risk of non-payment by customers rests with the customer-courier transaction and is not the liability of Yalla Venao.

---

## 5. Vendor Responsibilities

### 5.1 Licenses & Legal Compliance

The Vendor agrees to:
- Maintain all required business registrations, operating licenses, and permits under Panamanian law
- Comply with all applicable food safety, hygiene, and health regulations in the Republic of Panama
- Obtain and maintain any required health permits from the Ministry of Health (MINSA) or applicable authority

> ⚠️ Vendor failure to maintain required licenses is grounds for immediate suspension from the Platform.

### 5.2 Accurate Menu Listings

The Vendor agrees to:
- Keep all menu items, descriptions, prices, and availability up to date on the Platform
- Promptly notify Yalla Venao of menu changes, price updates, or item unavailability
- Clearly indicate any allergens, ingredients of concern, or dietary information for listed items
- Not list items they are unable or unauthorized to sell

> ⚠️ Vendors are solely responsible for the accuracy of their menu content. Yalla Venao is not responsible for customer complaints arising from inaccurate menu descriptions.

### 5.3 Order Fulfillment & Timely Preparation

The Vendor agrees to:
- Prepare orders with care, in a timely manner consistent with the estimated preparation time communicated to the Platform
- Notify the Platform immediately if an order cannot be fulfilled (out of stock, kitchen closure, etc.)
- Maintain consistent quality and portion sizes as described on the Platform
- Package orders safely for delivery

### 5.4 Food Safety

> ⚠️ **HIGH-RISK CLAUSE**

The Vendor is **solely responsible** for ensuring:
- All food products are safe for human consumption
- Proper food handling, storage, and preparation temperatures are maintained
- No contaminated, expired, or adulterated products are served
- Staff handling food comply with applicable hygiene standards

**Yalla Venao expressly disclaims any liability for foodborne illness, allergic reactions, or health issues arising from Vendor products.**

### 5.5 Professional Conduct

The Vendor agrees to treat Yalla Venao staff, couriers, and customers with professionalism and respect. Abusive, harassing, or discriminatory behavior is grounds for removal from the Platform.

## 6. Yalla Venao Responsibilities

Yalla Venao agrees to:
- List the Vendor's menu and business on the Platform in a timely and accurate manner following onboarding
- **Forward orders** to the Vendor via WhatsApp or the Platform's communication system
- Assign a courier to collect the order from the Vendor's location
- Provide basic **customer support** for order-related inquiries, routing complaints to the appropriate party (Vendor or courier) as applicable
- Provide the Vendor with periodic order summaries and commission statements
- Notify the Vendor in advance of any material changes to Platform policies or commission rates

## 11. Term & Termination

### 11.1 Term

This Agreement begins on the date signed (or the date the Vendor's listing goes live on the Platform, whichever is earlier) and continues until terminated by either Party.

### 11.2 Termination by Either Party

Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing **7 days' written notice** to the other party via email or WhatsApp message. No reason is required.

### 11.3 Immediate Termination for Cause

Yalla Venao may terminate this Agreement **immediately and without notice** if the Vendor:
- Violates food safety laws or poses a health risk to customers
- Engages in fraudulent conduct
- Harasses or abuses customers, couriers, or Platform staff
- Fails to pay commissions owed after 30 days past due

### 11.4 Effect of Termination

Upon termination, the Vendor's listing will be removed from the Platform. Any outstanding commissions owed at the time of termination remain due and payable within **14 days** of termination.
