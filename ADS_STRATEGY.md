# Yalla Venao — Paid & Organic Ads Strategy
**Version 1.0 | February 2026**
**Market: Playa Venao, Los Santos, Panama**

---

## ⚡ Strategic Context (Read First)

Playa Venao is not Panama City. It's a small surf beach village with an estimated 500–1,000 people in the area at any given time — and that number swells dramatically from **December through April (high season)**. This is not a mass-market play. It's a hyper-local, word-of-mouth-driven community where one well-placed Instagram reel or WhatsApp blast can convert faster than $1,000 in Facebook ads.

**Key strategic truths:**
- 🌊 **Seasonality is everything.** Launch hard before December. Wind down testing budgets in May–June. Use shoulder season (May–Nov) for brand building and vendor/courier recruitment.
- 📱 **Word-of-mouth is your #1 channel.** Every ad should push people to *try the app once*. One good experience creates 10 organic referrals in a town this size.
- 🌍 **Your customer speaks English first.** Tourists, surfers, and expats are the core market. Spanish secondary for locals and couriers.
- 📍 **Hyperlocal geo-targeting is non-negotiable.** Don't waste a dollar targeting Panama City. Every Meta/Google dollar should hit a 5–10km radius of Playa Venao.
- 🏪 **9 vendors is a selling point, not a limitation.** "All your favourite Venao spots, one app" is a real hook.

Don't over-invest here in Month 1.

## 3. CONVERSION FUNNELS

### Funnel A: Customer (Tourist / Expat)

```
AWARENESS
  ├─ Instagram/TikTok Reel → sees "order from the beach" content
  ├─ Google Display ad → "Playa Venao delivery app" remarketing
  └─ Word of mouth from other tourists at the hostel / surf camp

        ↓

INTEREST
  ├─ Clicks ad → lands on app landing page (yalla-venao.vercel.app)
  ├─ Sees vendor list: recognizes the restaurants they've already visited
  └─ Thinks: "I didn't know you could order from [place]"

        ↓

DESIRE
  ├─ Browses menu — sees real prices, photos
  ├─ Notices free download, no subscription
  └─ Remembers being hungry/tired/sunburned with no energy to walk

        ↓

CONVERSION
  ├─ Downloads app → creates account → places first order
  └─ Retargeting ad (if they visited site but didn't convert): discount on first order or social proof ad
```

**Landing Page:** App home page (yalla-venao.vercel.app). Must show: vendor logos, how it works (3 steps), and one-tap download button. **Remove all friction.** If there's no app store page yet, make this the conversion page itself with a "pre-register" or WhatsApp CTA.

**Retargeting sequence:**
- Day 1–3 after visit: "Still hungry? First delivery is waiting." (urgency)
- Day 4–7: "Here's what people are ordering on Yalla Venao" (social proof carousel)

```
AWARENESS
  ├─ Facebook feed ad (Spanish) → sees "earn more from tourists" hook
  ├─ WhatsApp forward from another vendor who joined
  └─ Instagram post/story from @YallaVenao tagging local businesses

        ↓

INTEREST
  ├─ Clicks ad → WhatsApp chat opens OR lands on vendor info page
  ├─ Reads: 0 upfront, 15% only on completed orders
  └─ Thinks: "I lose nothing by trying"

DESIRE
  ├─ Sees social proof: "9 local businesses already using it"

### Funnel C: Courier (Local Motorbike Rider)

```
AWARENESS
  ├─ Facebook/Instagram ad (Spanish, local) → income opportunity

### When to Scale vs. Kill

**Scale when:**
- Customer CPA < $5 AND you can track that first orders are repeating (LTV signal)
- Any ad set delivers CTR > 2.5% — double the budget weekly (max 2x per week)
- A specific creative clearly outperforms — cut others, scale the winner
- High season (Dec–Apr): increase customer budget by 2–3x; reduce vendor/courier (you should have supply by then)

**Kill / Pause when:**
- Spending $50+ with 0 conversions in first 3 days (creative problem — swap immediately)
- CPM > $25 (audience too small/competitive — broaden or shift to different placement)
- An ad set has spent 2x your target CPA with 0 conversions — pause it
- Low season (May–Nov): pause customer acquisition paid ads. Focus budget on organic + vendor/courier recruitment for next season.

**Seasonality calendar:**
```
Jan–Apr:   🟢 Full customer acquisition spend. Scale winners aggressively.
May–Jun:   🟡 Ramp down customer budget. Run vendor/courier recruitment.
Jul–Nov:   🟡 Organic only (or minimal $5/day retargeting). Build supply side.
Dec:       🟢 Relaunch customer campaigns with fresh creatives. High-season blitz.
```
